## Parameter perturbations in a post-treatment chronic myeloid leukemia model capture the essence of pre-diagnosis A-bomb survivor mysteries. 

Radivoyevitch, T. Radiat Environ Biophys (2020).

This paper shows that the model of Hahnel et al (adjacent folder) is consistent 
with two CML anomalies of A-bomb survivor data, delays in onsets in Hiroshima
and  absence in Nagasaki adults. Scripts here reproduce figures in this paper.
