Model of   [Craig et al  *Bull Math Biol* **78** 2304-2357 (2016)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27324993) in the R package myelo with chemo doses accessed via shinyapp.io. 

Move the *Dose* per cycle slider to see the effects of 6 pulsed cycles of the 
marrow toxic chemotherapeutic agent Zalypsis. 
Note: simulations, and thus figure updates, take ~20 seconds.
